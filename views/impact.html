The impact Bob will make!

Can he fix it?  Yes! He Can!
Bob has been designed with current problems in the prespective by us, here is a list of impactful changes he will bring to the current situation of society!

1

Crack detection

Extra resources that go into the rebuilding of the structure due to late realisation of these cracks can easily be avoided as "BOB" would check out the site on a daily basis according to the requirement.

2

Vibration Analysis

To a naked eye, buildings and bridges appear to be fixed in place,unmoved by forces like wind and rain.But,in fact these large structures do experience imperceptibly small vibrations and indicates instability.
BOB is capable of reducing this collapse of structure by detection of the Vibrations using Eulerian Algorithm. Thereby reducing structural collapses and reducing risk on the lives of the people.

3

Spo2 Estimation

Clever Solution to the problem of Covid 19 and also to the other diseases those are detectable by heart rate and elevated body temperature. A great way to check workers health status without any other human contact. Hence an win- win situation .

4

Chatbot

Providing easy to use interactivity will incorporate Bob to manage a lot of daily task handling. It can work on attendance and even call emergency services in accident!

5

Attraction

Bob can serve as a strong attraction point for the builders to showcase their projects to intrested parties coming to inspect the buildings and other structures!
