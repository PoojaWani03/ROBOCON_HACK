The plan of action for further implementation of our idea

  • Targeting Hospitals and Construction sites

    Planning on getting a first hand report on the idea

    Spo2 data collection from various hospitals and approaching construction sites to have our idea solidified through prototyping and testing.

  • Testing Our prototype

    Platform to test our software prototype on pre provided bot

    Platform like the DWS will help us run our software model on their bots, to get a clear picture on what can be intensified and nullified in the exsisting idea.

  • Transition to Full Service

    Path after a successful prototyping

    Once the idea comes accross as a impactful one, the bot "BOB THE BUILDER" can be set out as a service robot for construction arenas by seeking investments from group of Builders/constructers.
