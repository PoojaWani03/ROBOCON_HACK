Marketing Strategy

The plan of action for further implementation of our idea in the targeted industry

  • Targeting Hospitals and Construction sites

    Planning on getting a firsthand report on the idea

    Spo2 data collection from various hospitals and approaching construction sites to have our idea solidified through prototyping and testing.

  • Testing Our prototype

    Platform to test our software prototype on pre- provided bot

    Platform like the DWS Global might help us run our software model on their bots and expand our experience,to get a clear picture on what can be intensified and nullified in the existing idea.

  • Transition to Full Service

    Path after a successful prototyping!

    Once the idea comes across as an impactful one, the bot "BOB THE BUILDER" can be set out as a service robot for construction areas by seeking investments from group of Builders/constructors.

  • Expansion

    Phase Two

    Licensing the model and thereby converting it to a full scale product along with service(depends on the success rate) and improving on the production aspect of the model
